How It Works

For Stakers

You stake MOR on a builder subnet. Your MOR is locked for 7 days. You earn the builder's native project tokens, not MOR. Each project decides its own distribution schedule. Some distribute immediately, others have a bootstrap period.

For Builders

When people stake MOR on your subnet, your project earns a proportional share of the network's builder emissions pool (currently ~{{DAILY_EMISSIONS}} MOR/day, set by the protocol). Those MOR emissions go to your project's AMM liquidity pool (50% swapped for your token, paired as protocol-owned liquidity).

Staking vs Inference

Holding MOR = compute access (stake MOR to open AI inference sessions).
Staking on builders = supporting projects and earning their tokens. These are separate mechanisms.
