How Builder Staking Works

Stakers: Lock MOR on a subnet for 7 days. Earn the builder's native project tokens (not MOR). Each project sets its own token schedule.

Builders: MOR staked on your subnet earns a proportional share of the network's builder emissions pool (currently ~{{DAILY_EMISSIONS}} MOR/day, set by the protocol), added to your project's AMM liquidity pool.

Separate from inference. Holding MOR = compute access (stake it to open AI inference sessions). Staking on builders = supporting projects.
