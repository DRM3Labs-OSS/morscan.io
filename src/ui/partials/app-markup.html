How Staking Works

Lock MOR as collateral to open an AI session. Your MOR is returned when the session ends - locked, not spent. The only cost is ETH gas.

Session Lifecycle

Open → Lock MOR. Active → Provider serves inference. Close → Provider signs receipt. Reclaim → MOR returned.
